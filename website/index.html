IV, EV and Stats Calculator

For core games

Description

This app is a calculator for the stats on the Pokemon core games on the consoles.

Calculate the IV, EV and Stats of a Pokemon. It supports up to 8th Gen. Pokemon.


You have to select the Pokemon, the Nature and the Level. Then:

  • The IVs are calculated by the Pokemon's Stats and the EVs.
  • The EVs are calculated by the Pokemon's Stats and the IVs.
  • The Stats are calculated by the Pokemon's IVs and EVs.

Select and evolve Pokemons in an informed and confident way.
